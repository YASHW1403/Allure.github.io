BODY MASS INDEX (BMI)


Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. Calculate your BMI to determine your goal.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5 (Gain weight)
  • Normal weight: 18.5 < BMI < 24.9 (Maintain)
  • Overweight: BMI > 24.9 (Loss weight)

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


If you have a Question Regarding workouts, you can contact us but firstly you should browse our FAQ section.


Contact Us


  • How often should I exercise?

    In general, it is recommended that you start with 3 exercise sessions a week. Over time, you should progress to 6 days a week.

  • Are rest days important?

    Rest days are super important because they allow your body the chance to recover, repair and rebuild.

  • Should I skip cardio?

    Cardio not only exercises the working muscles, but it also works the heart and vascular system. It increases physical work capacity, the ability to work or play, But in general, the advice is to do cardio at the start of your workout if you're trying to build stamina (it acts as a warm-up)

  • How can I lose fat quickly?

    If your goal is to lose weight quickly and you are short on time, an efficient way is to do HIIT workouts. (High Intensity Interval Training).

  • can I get abs fast?

    Nope! Back in the day, I thought if you wanted a six-pack you needed to work out your abs every day.

  • Can you turn fat into muscle?

    No! Muscles burn fat for energy. Building muscle boosts your metabolism.
